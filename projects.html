    • 2023-04-30 Low-Force Joystick

    In BME350 we were tasked by our client CanAssist to desgin a low-force josytick that could be used by users who have muscular dystrophy or other conditions that could hinder their ability to generate adequate force required by an average game joystick. This design was constructed using simple components salvaged from an arduino joystick such as the potentiometers with the body and other components being made from PLA via 3D-printing. The inner spring was formed using TPU via 3D-printing.
